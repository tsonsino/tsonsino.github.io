AI Toolkit HR tools I built

Most of my career has been spent inside lean, fast-scaling companies — full-time and as a consultant, often building HR from scratch. The same problems kept showing up: managers avoiding hard conversations because nobody gave them a framework, calibration sessions that produced paperwork instead of decisions, headcount plans built on gut feel. I built these tools to address that gap. They are not meant to write the review or run the session — they are meant to slow a manager down long enough to actually think. AI is not here to replace that thinking — it is here to prompt it, structure it, and make it easier to act on. The goal is the same thing I have always tried to do as an HRBP: give managers the structure to do the people part of their job better, and give lean HR teams the leverage to focus on what actually matters.
